Conclusion

What does this mean for Simple Random Sampling?

BIAS: SRS is UNBIASED because any plot can be chosen at random. Every plot has an equal chance.
VARIABILITY: SRS has HIGH VARIATION. If we sample again, we get a totally different number. One sample might be high, another low.
VERDICT: SRS is FAIR but INCONSISTENT. It's unbiased on average, but any single sample might be far from the truth by random chance.
