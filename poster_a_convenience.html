Conclusion

What does this mean for Convenience Sampling?

BIAS: This method is BIASED due to UNDERCOVERAGE. We systematically excluded plots near the river which had higher yields.
VARIABILITY: Low variability, but in a bad way‚Äîour estimates are consistently wrong in the same direction.
VERDICT: Convenience sampling is fast and cheap, but NOT RECOMMENDED when the population has systematic differences across locations.
